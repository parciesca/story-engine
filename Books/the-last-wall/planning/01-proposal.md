CHAPTER HANDOFF
  Type: multiple-choice
  Written: 2026-04-25

  The walls held their first day. The masons filled the breach before dark.
  Tomorrow the cannon fires again.

  A. Aleksios goes home. His mother, Eleni, is preparing the house as if nothing
     is happening — or as if everything is, and that is no reason to stop. The
     conversation about the galley passages happens whether he wants it to or not.

  B. Giustiniani summons Aleksios before dawn. A Genoese trader in Galata — the
     nominally neutral colony across the Golden Horn — has been receiving messages
     from someone inside the Ottoman camp. Giustiniani wants Aleksios to cross
     over and make contact. The man's name is Demetrios. He speaks no Turkish.

  C. The Emperor's chamberlain finds Aleksios at the gate. Constantine XI wants
     to know what Minotto actually said — not the diplomatic version, the real one.
     A private audience in the Blachernae palace, the last imperial court in the world.
