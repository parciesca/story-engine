  You stand at the split, rope over one shoulder, Marta humming behind you.

  A. Take the left arch — finer stonework, geometric carvings, where the Fletcher twins went and came back changed and wealthy. Marta did say it was faster, whatever that means.

  B. Head right into the rough-hewn tunnel — wider, a later addition, where Bastwick found his allegedly fish-whispering mushroom. Unknown quantity in both directions.

  C. Follow the main path straight ahead — the known route, past the baker's root cellar, and then into whatever comes after the turnips.
